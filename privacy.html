Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Thank you for visiting onememorydesign.com. Protecting your personal data is important to us. Below, we explain what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

This privacy policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant data protection laws.

2. Who is responsible for data processing?

Jennifer Wohlfahrtstätter & Patrick Fuchs
Email: onememorydesign@gmail.com

3. What data do we collect and why?

Automatically collected data (server log files)

When you visit our website, certain data is automatically collected by our web server for technical and security reasons:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL (previously visited page)
  • Date and time of access

This data is not merged with other data sources and is processed based on legitimate interest (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR) to ensure website functionality and security.

Data you provide (e.g., via email or contact forms)

If you contact us via email or other means, we process the data you provide (e.g., name, email, message) for communication purposes. This is based on your consent (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR) or necessary for fulfilling a contract (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR).

4. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure basic functionality. These are stored based on legitimate interest (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR).

We do not use third-party tracking or marketing cookies.

You can configure your browser to reject cookies, but this may affect website functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook/Instagram plugins
  • Third-party advertising or tracking tools

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR) – Know what data we store about you.
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) – Correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) – Request deletion of your data.
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR) – Limit how we use your data.
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR) – Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR) – Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting past processing).

To exercise your rights, contact us at onememorydesign@gmail.com.

If you believe your data is being misused, you can file a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde).

7. Data Security

We use SSL encryption to protect your data and take appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure stored information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Please check back periodically for any changes.
